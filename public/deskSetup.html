TOP DESK SETUP IDEAS

Comfort is key when it comes to spending hours in front of your computer, especially if you are working from home. Investing in office desk setup ideas is worth each penny because the result will increased your productivity and boost your energy level. A productive workspace doesn't have to be expensive. The new trend is a MINIMALIST DESK SETUP which happens to be my favorite. Less clutter more thinking. more clutter messy thoughts. But, at the end of the day it is up to you how you want your set up to be. So, this article will cover some of the best desk setup ideas that falls right under your budget.

1. CLEAN DESK SETUP

Beautiful white setup with a little hint of green brings a clam and relaxing vibe to your coding session. A minimalist desk setup helps with clearer thinking. It is said that "the cleaner your work area the clearer your thoughts". I always recommend any sort of plant on the desk or near the desk area to help reduce stress and clean air.

2. CONTENT CREATOR DESK SETUP

If you a programmer as well as a content creator, then this type of setup will be perfect for you. A simple desk that that would allow you to mount different equipments like Microphone, Camera, Lights etc.. on it. The white background is very clean... The double monitors is great developers who usually has multiple tabs open when they are coding, gaming, or editing.

3. NATURE DESK SETUP

If you are a naturist like me then this is the perfect setup for you. All you need is a dedicated wall with some inexpensive plants to brighten your day. I highly recommend buying plants that is beneficial to your health. For instance, Snake plants helps you breed better, Spider Plant helps Purifies indoor air by removing harmful chemicals, Peace Lily Purifies indoor air and can absorb acetone vapors. This setup would not only ease the tension from coding but when you need to take a moment to regain your thoughts, watering the plants will definitely help.

4. COUPLES DESK SETUP

This setup is perfect for couples or if your a developer who enjoy gaming. Yes, it would be easier to use just one monitor however, some monitor has different refresh rate therefore, gaming on a monitor design for gaming will definitely gives you a different experience. It is also cleaner to have your gadgets away from the desk top. The shelves and pegboard behind and over the computer is a perfect idea to store items. The black walls complements the wood top desk, giving a garage slash office feel.
