TOP 10 GADGET TO MAKE YOUR DESK AREA COMFORTABLE

A Programmer's job require a lot of focus and can be very challenging. So, surrounding yourself with the best possible gadgets will keep you healthy and increase productivity. It does not matter if you are working in your office or at home, have these gadgets around and in your coding space will make your job fun and easier.

1. RUBIK'S CUBE

Rubik Cube is one of the best gadgets you can have on your computer desk. Rubik Cube helped train and strengthen the brain cell. Not only that this piece of gadget can on turn your thinking light on is also helps ease the tension for coding. As a programmer, we gets frustrated when our code is not compiling or giving the output as we wanted. It is necessary to relax the brain but stepping away from the computer

2. HOUR GLASS

Hour glass is another great gadget to keep on your desk. Use an hour glass if you want to train yourself to code within a specific time. For instance, if your are learning a new programing Language try coding a small project before the sand is empty from the upper bulb.

3. CAMERA

If you are a web developer or planing to go into web development I highly suggest that you take up the hobbies of being a PHOTOGRAPHER. In order to have visitors to stay on your page it must be interactive, and must have a reason for the visitors to stay. And, the best way to keep visitors on you website you must have Pictures. Even if you are building a website for clients it is always best to have you own photos than to use someone elses photos online.

4. FITNESS TRACKER

Programmers!! Please take care of your health. It is the most important trait to have as a computer programmer. Take a break sometimes to engage in some type of mindfulness. You can purchase a fitbit or an apple watch to track your fitness and wellness. The fitness apps made for these watches are great. Wether if it is jogging, breathing, or mindfulness, please take sometime of coding for health and wellness.

5. Arduino

Get hands on and interactive with you code. If you are into Software Creation, you can download you C++, C, PYTHON or JAVA code to the iRobot any enjoy you creation!

6. Headphone

Weather you are into music or high frequency vibration sound to active the brain cell, Or even to catch a vibe while coding. I recommend getting a noise cancellation headphones enhance you of music. Not only will you enjoy great sound but you will also enjoy not hearing noisy family members or outside sound.

7. ROCKETBOOK SMART NOTEBOOK

As a programmer, we are constantly think about the next big ideas for website, projects and apps! Most of this ideas vanish within an hour, the best practice is to write down your ideas. And I highly recommend reusable paper. The ROCKETBOOK SMART NOTEBOOK is a very inexpensive gadget and completely reusable. Once you are done with a page, yon can scan it using the app and then wipe the down off for next use.

8. Inspire Relaxing Candle

Inspire Relaxing candle is a non-tech addition to your desk, the scent will help to ease your stressed mind and allow you to focus. The Inspire Candle comes in a sleek white dish to instantly fit into your workspace. It’s sleek and stylish, just as you’d expect with any other Mac accessory. The candle combines bergamot, amber, tarragon, and musk. These scent profiles all have beneficial effects on your brain. Once you light it, you’ll feel the calming effects and be able to fly through your to-do list.

9. LED LIGHTINGS

LED lights brings relaxation and coziness to your coding space. You can purchase some inexpensive lights on amazon for about $15-50 and stick it on the back of you computer desk. A nice interchangeable colors to match your mood would make your coding session much more intense.

10. FOOT STAND

When sitting at a computer desk for a long period of time, it is very important that you make sure that the blood circulate through your foot properly. Footrest also help reduce back strain, pressure on the legs, reduce fatigue, and ease pain or discomfort in the feet, ankles, and knees.
