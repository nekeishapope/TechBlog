5 BEST MONITOR FOR PROGRAMMING

Any MONITOR will work for coding. It is not needed to purchase the most expensive Monitor to create some beautiful, creative projects. However, when your are spending long hours on the computer coding, it is recommended that you get a screen that will be beneficial to your eyes, and multiple or wide screen for better productivity. Having monitor space is very important for every programmer. We often have multiple tabs and applications open at the same time on our computer. We will have visual studio open and a web browser open for testing, and maybe another tab open for research.

1. Apple Studio Display

The apple monitor is a beautiful solid 27 inch 5k screen. The microphone, speaker, and webcam are the best i've ever seen on any monitor. The microphone and speakers are very loud and clear. There's an entire A13 Bionic chip powering the audio and camera system, same as the iPhone 11, and the camera itself is a 12-megapixel sensor with an f/2.4 lens.

Features

Resolution: 5120 x 2880
Refresh Rate: 60Hz
Inputs: 1 thunderbolt 4 port, and 3 USB-C port Connectivity: USB-C

2. LG 34WN80C-B UltraWide Curved Monitor

The lg ultra wide curve monitor is the perfect monitor for PROGRAMMING because it can do the work of two monitors. With its ultra-wide display, the 34-inch screen comes with more than enough real estate for all of your work. It has solid speaker. The best speaker you can get on a monitor and even on a computer.

Features

Resolution: 2560 x 1080
Refresh Rate: 75Hz
Inputs: 1x DisplayPort, 1x HDMI Connectivity: USB-C

3. Dell UltraSharp U2720Q 27" 4K

This monitor a great second monitor for programmers because of it's interchangeability or swivel between vertical and horizontal easily. This monitor has USB-C connectivity and put 90watts power delivery. It acts as a USB-C hub and a charger so you can charge your laptop whenever you plug it into the monitor. I currently use this monitor as my vertical screen when I am writing over 500 lines of codes which I find very helpful.

Features

Resolution: 3840 x 2160
Refresh Rate: 60 Hz
Inputs: 2 USB-C, 3 USB 3.1 ports, 2 HDMI ports, 1 audio line out

4. HUAWEI MateView GT Ultrawide Curved Monitor

The Huawei MateView is a ultrawide Curved Monitor with a smooth refresh rate of 165Hz. It has a 3k resolution VA panel with hi color accuracy. It also has an adjustable RGB soundbar stand with touch control. The monitor is powered by a 135w USB-C adaptor.

Features

Resolution: 3440 x 1440
Refresh: 165Hz
Inputs: 2 HDMI, 1 USB-C DisplayPort
